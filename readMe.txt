Project Name: MineSweeper
Date: December 9, 2022
Creators: Llum Acosta, Alston Chen

1. Go into the startMenu.java file and click run.
2. In the interface, select difficulty and gamemode. This chooses the board size and number of mines
3. Click the play button.
4. Proceed with playing minesweeper.
5. Click a button.
6. The number tells you how many mines are near that cell.
7. Continue, don't click on a bomb.
8. You win if you click every cell/button without a mine.
